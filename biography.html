Biography

I am currently a first year Ph.D. candidate at IRCAM/UPMC, in the music representation team. I am working under the co-supervision of Philippe Esling, Florent Jacquemard, and Geoffroy Peeters. Our work focuses on machine learning, more specifically on generative models for time series and its application to automatic musical orchestration (Ph.D. subject).

I received the State Engineering degree from the École Télécom Paristech, France, in 2016, and the M.Sc. degree in computer sciences from Université Pierre et Marie-Curie, France, in 2015
